1. Competitive audit goal(s)
Compare the user experience of each competitor’s website and app
	 
2. Who are your key competitors? (Description)
Our key competitors are:
 
-      Ecofin. They are a small business stationed in Hyderabad, Telangana. Ecofin is a fintech company facilitating personal loans through a mobile application to salaried employees pan-India, within 24 hours.
-      Lazypay is India's new age digital credit provider. It truly is the fastest way to get credit in a single tap. LazyPay's One tap digital checkout process is available at over 250+ leading apps in India including the likes of Swiggy, Zomato, Book My Show, Make My Trip, Vodafone, TataSky and many more!
-      MoneyTap is India’s 1st Credit Line App | Borrow up to ₹5 Lakh | Partnered with banks & NBFCs regulated by the RBI.
-          Avant is a credit-first financial technology company* that provides access to innovative financial solutions, including personal loans and credit cards, that meet customers’ needs wherever they are on their financial journey.
	 
	 
 
 
 
 
3. What are the type and quality of competitors’ products? (Description)
Ecofin facilitates personal loans through a mobile application to salaried employees across India within 24 hours.
MoneyTap provides a personal line of credit of up to 5 lakhs online.
LazyPay provides merchant checkout, personal loans, and billpay.
Avant facilitates personal loans and credit cards.
 
	 
4. How do competitors position themselves in the market?  (Description)
Ecofin positions itself as a fintech tech company providing loans to millions of salaried employees.
MoneyTap positions itself as India’s first personal line of credit.
LazyPay positions itself as India’s credit super-app.
Avant positions itself as a firm that helps personnel move financially forward.
 
	 
 
5. Competitors’ strengths (List)

Avant’s strengths
-          Provides Personal Loans
-          Provides a Credit Card
 
	 
 
 
 
 
6. Competitors’ weaknesses (List)
Ecofin’s weaknesses
-      Another loan cannot be provided until the current one is completed
-      Logo design does not communicate the company's ethos.
-      Having a dedicated company app
-       Users have to download a PDF of the menu to view it
MoneyTap’s weaknesses
-      For loan amounts less than Rs 10,000, the only EMI option available is for 2 months.
-      Menu is overwhelming
-      Inconsistent fonts
LazyPay’s weaknesses
-      The personal loan at LazyPay is available at an interest rate of 18%, which is at the higher end.
-      Logo design does not communicate the company's ethos
Avant’s weaknesses
-      Feels dense and overwhelming in places
-      A lot of text for basic issues
