Profile.fyi is the next-gen professional social network aimed at enabling people to build their ultimate career profile by showcasing things that matter.
Who are we looking for?
We are looking for a full-time Frontend Engineer and a few interns based in India (preferably Bangalore). You will be responsible for designing and implementing user interfaces, testing your written code, and debugging whenever required. Given that we are a distributed team, you should be great with collaborating and communicating with other engineers and founders to ensure projects meet specifications.
Your Responsibilities
* Build a delightful product and user experience
* Work with the founders and designers to develop, deliver, and ship features
* Make the web responsive, efficient, and performant without compromising on speed or quality
Your Qualifications and Role Requirements
* Curious with a high bias for action
* Hands-on experience with (NextJS or SvelteKit) and (TailwindCSS)
* Deep understanding of JavaScript.
* Knowledge of functional programming.
* Great with communication both written and verbal (given we are a remote team)
Interview Process
* You will submit your application by completing the assignment (linked below)
* Interview round 1 / 60 minutes / Hands-on Coding Round - Extension to Assignment
* Interview round 2 / 45 minutes / Resume, Projects, and Profile discussion
* Interview round 3 / 45 minutes / Culture round and expectation settings
* Offer letter.
Each round will be a filter for the next round, and you will receive an interview call only if your submission is up to the mark. The entire process will be completed within a week.
