🏪 About Kirana Club
We're Kirana Club, and we're on a mission to revolutionise how FMCG companies connect with India's 15 million Kirana stores.
We are building 'LinkedIn for Kiranas' and have over 2M Kiranas on our digital network. Leading FMCG brands like Mondelez, Marico, PepsiCo, Coca-Cola, Cycle Pure, Wipro, Britannia, and Adani partner with Kirana Club to launch products, promote brands, and gain real-time market insights.
