QuillAudits UI/UX Design Intern Task
Task Overview:
As a UI/UX Design Intern at QuillAudits, you're at the forefront of shaping the user experience for our web3 security platform. Your task is to conceptualize and design an innovative, user-centric feature for our website that reflects QuillAudits’ commitment to security and education in the web3 space.
Assignment 1: Redesign the Product Ecosystem Section
Objective:
Improve the visual appeal, functionality, and user experience of the "Product Ecosystem" section on our website, ensuring it effectively showcases our range of security tools for various niches (B2B, B2C, D2C).
Deliverables:
* Wireframes: Sketch or Figma wireframes showcasing the new layout and design elements.
* Prototype (Optional): A clickable prototype demonstrating the user interaction and flow.
* Design Mockup: High-fidelity design mockup of the redesigned section.
* Design Rationale Document: A brief explanation of your design choices, focusing on how they enhance user experience and align with web3 cybersecurity themes.
Assignment 2: Conceptualize a User Onboarding Experience
Objective:
Design a scenario-based onboarding experience for new users visiting the QuillAcademy platform. The goal is to engage users from the moment they land on the page, guiding them through the various learning paths and resources available. (For eg Different Youtube Playlists, Github Repos, Articles, CTFs)
Deliverables:
* User Flow Diagram: Illustrate the onboarding process from landing to choosing a learning path.
* Low-Fidelity Wireframes: Outline the key screens involved in the onboarding experience.
* High-Fidelity Mockup (Optional): A detailed mockup of one or two key screens in the onboarding flow.
* Onboarding Strategy Document: Describe the strategy behind your onboarding design, including how it introduces users to QuillAudits Academy and encourages engagement.
General Requirements:
* Ensure the designs are visually appealing and reflect QuillAudits' brand identity as a web3 cybersecurity firm.
* Designs should prioritize ease of navigation, performance optimization, and responsiveness across devices.
* Incorporate elements that highlight the security aspect of our offerings.
* The design should not alter the existing branding, color palette, or font family but should creatively work within these parameters.
Submission Guidelines:
* Combine all deliverables into a single PDF document (if multiple files are necessary, provide a compressed folder).
* Include your name and the assignment title in the document/folder name.
* Email your submission to hr@quillhash.com within 4 days of receiving this task.
Disclaimer:
Attention to detail in terms of cleanliness, the naming of frames, and the structure of components is crucial. Documentation of your design decisions and rationalization plays a significant role in evaluating your submission.
