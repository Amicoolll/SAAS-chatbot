About DeepThought
DeepThought is a DeepTech Innovation company working in the education space. We build products basis empirical research on socio-psychological first principles of learning. DeepThought's technological product innovation aims at transforming a normal classroom into a research classroom. DeepThought was founded by Tarun, a Computer Scientist, and TEDx speaker with 10 years of field experience in the education space in 150+ schools.
