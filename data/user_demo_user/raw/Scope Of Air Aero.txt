Reward: Mobile app 
Description: We have created a product that will provide the reward to customers and customers can use those rewards. Product will take the Customer info and that info will save in the Air Aero DB. 


Solution: We will be developing 2 Platforms, App(iOS, Android) and Admin(Web Panel) panel. 


Application: Used by customers.
Admin: Used by the Air Aero Team.
