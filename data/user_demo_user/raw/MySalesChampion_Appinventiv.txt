Project Understanding
Proposed Platform “Mysales Champion” will be a marketplace that will allow Companies to post their products to the platform. They will be able to sign a contract and can buy subscription within the platform. User will be able to get the customized JS Snippet. Companies will also be able to access their Dashboard and can manage their profile, Accounts, Notifications and settings.
Salesperson Will be able to Browse Different Opportunities available on the platform, They will be able to Add the products to their offer page, Salesperson will be able to accept/Un-Accept a product. Salesperson will be able to access their Dashboard where they can view various reporting and sales.

Girls Trade's platform enables women to buy or rent clothing at the fraction of the retail price by tapping into the communities existing wardrobes. This sharing economy breaks down the barriers of access, cost and fast fashion. The economy, social media, content creators and fast fashion have created an environment of social pressure to be the ‘best’ version of yourself. For women specifically, this is especially reflected in fashion. Being on trend with the latest trends and looking your best at all time is expensive and time-consuming!


We're making fashion work for you by enabling members to post their entire wardrobe on our marketplace for free. Once uploaded other members will have the option to rent or buy items, depending on what the seller listed the item as. Our marketplace allows you to find items ranging from expensive designer clothing to some old sneakers, you need an outfit? The Girls Trade community has your back. Members will also be able to post a "Looking For" advert where you can ask the community for a specific item, specific category or even advice on an outfit!
