VISION


IKON  / イ コ ン  NFT aims to be a brand known through-out the world for it’s benchmark setting products and utility, accessible not only to the Web3 space but to everyone. Through IKON, we will bridge the gap between the whole world and Web3 by making our products well-renowned outside the space while maintaining engaging and beneficial utility for our valuable holders as well.




The IKON manga, made accessible and known in the entire world because of its interesting plot, will serve as a source of positive mass attraction to the project. We will also be creating an innovative experience of being able to own the NFT of a character from the manga/anime and see it react as the story progresses. The community will be able to place their bets on a character’s development in the story by bidding on the character’s NFT.




IKON will be setting up the foundation and introduction of a brand new niche ~ ‘V-IDOLS’,  3D models that represent a unique identity in the IKON-VERSE allowing the user to socialize and interact with others while being able to express their personality with perfection through the action-imitating features of the model. We also intend to provide a Web3 integrated Karaoke platform for the ‘V-IDOLS’ allowing them to stream, practice and create personal spaces called ‘koncerts’ where the users can perform Karaoke together with the help of their ‘V-IDOLS’.


We also aim to drive Web3 towards mass adoption by onboarding the Anime and Pop industry through the attraction driven by our famous products falling in their niche. We will be educating them about Web3 through regular ‘manga guide chapters’ on buying, holding, or selling their NFT/crypto safely.     




Simply put, IKON intends to be one of the top projects in the space, creating not just for the Web3 community but for everyone. We’ll be the bridge linking our space with the Anime and Pop industry facilitating Web3 adoption in the most effective way.
